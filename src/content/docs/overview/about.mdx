This guide helps beginners get started with data science and analytics. It presents clear, practical steps and examples so learners can build the skills needed for datathons, personal projects, and entry-level analytics work.

## Who this is for

- Newcomers to data science or analytics
- Students preparing for datathons or hackathons
- Practitioners looking for a concise reference of common workflows
- Anyone who prefers hands-on, example-driven learning
